I'm a data engineer who believes working with tech should be easy. Each day, there is plenty happening at the fusion of Data x Computing. We have tooling options to solve things we couldn't conceive of five years ago.

My page attempts to follow those developments and considers where we're heading next. A challenge for the profession now is to keep track of change: to design systems both stable enough to perform, but flexible enough to adapt should better solutions arrive.

Datolya is the Hungarian word for the date fruit. There was an old social network site where we had to raise camels and feed them dates to make them gain superpowers. I found it a nice metaphor for what data allows companies to do.

My background is in Finance and Central Banking. Since then, I spent years at major tech companies where I developed the data architecture to work with payment systems (Apple, Google, Stripe, PayPal, Adyen ...)
