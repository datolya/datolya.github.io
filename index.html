I'm a data engineer stuck with the idea that working with tech should be easy.

There is plenty going on at the fusion of Data x Computing. My page follows those developments and asks where we're heading next. I find that our profession is often slow when forced to react to change. It is an interesting challenge to design systems which are performant, but also flexible to adapt should the better solution be published tomorrow.

Datolya is the Hungarian word for the date fruit. There was an old social network site where you could raise camels and feed it to them to make them gain superpowers. I found it a nice metaphor for what data allows companies to do.

My background is in finance and central banking. Since then, I spent years in tech building data integration with major payment systems (Apple, Google, Stripe, PayPal, Adyen ...)
