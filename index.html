I'm a data professional stuck with the idea that working with tech should feel intuitive. The best analytics stands out because it brings together neat statistical tricks with smart engineering.

There's plenty happening at the intersection of Data x Computing. My page follows those developments and asks where we're heading next. I've found our profession often struggles to proactively anticipate change. It's an interesting problem to design systems that perform well today, but remain flexible enough to adapt if the better solution drops tomorrow.

datolya is the Hungarian word for the date fruit. There used to be a social network site where you could raise camels and feed them dates to give them superpowers. I thought of it as a fitting metaphor for what data allows us to do.

My background is in finance and central banking. I've also spent years supporting large-scale tech acquisitions and building the data integration for industry leading payment systems.
